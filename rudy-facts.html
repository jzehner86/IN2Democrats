Votes Against Our Values

Voted Yea — Limit, Save, Grow Act

Slashing the Social Safety Net

Yakym voted for legislation that would enact devastating cuts to Medicaid, SNAP (food assistance), and other vital social safety programs that thousands of Hoosier families rely on—all to finance tax cuts for the wealthiest Americans.

The Impact: Jeopardizes healthcare access and food security for Indiana's most vulnerable populations, including children and seniors.

Sources: H.R. 2811 — Congress.gov · House Roll Call Vote #199 (Apr. 26, 2023)

Voted Nay — Protecting America's Workforce Act

Undermining Workers' Rights

Consistently voting against working people, Yakym opposed legislation designed to restore and protect collective bargaining rights for over a million workers. He consistently backs corporate interests over the rights of union members and everyday employees.

The Impact: Weakens the ability of Hoosier workers to negotiate for fair wages, safe working conditions, and reasonable benefits.

Sources: H.R. 2550 — Congress.gov · AFL-CIO Scorecard

Voted Yea — H.R. 1 (Polluters Over People Act)

Rolling Back Clean Water Safeguards

Yakym voted to prioritize corporate polluters over public health by supporting legislation that repeals critical clean water safeguards and environmental protections, threatening the rivers, lakes, and drinking water of the 2nd District.

The Impact: Lowers the standard for clean drinking water and exposes Indiana communities to unnecessary environmental risks.

Sources: H.R. 1 — Congress.gov · House Roll Call Vote #182 (Mar. 30, 2023) · LCV Scorecard

Voted Yea — Anti-Choice Appropriations Amendments

Attacking Reproductive Freedom

Yakym has consistently voted to restrict reproductive healthcare access. He supported amendments targeting the FDA's ability to approve and distribute mifepristone, and voted to defund vital reproductive health programs.

The Impact: Strips away fundamental healthcare decisions from Hoosier women and targets medical experts for their scientific decision-making.

Sources: Reproductive Freedom for All Scorecard · Full Voting Record — Congress.gov
